# FAQ - Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about building and launching the ADHD Flow OS.

## General Questions

### What is ADHD Flow OS?
A complete Notion-based life management system designed specifically for ADHD brains, featuring 250+ AI prompts, dopamine-optimized design, and comprehensive workbooks.

### Who is this for?
Adults 25-45 who are diagnosed or self-identify as ADHD (or AuDHD), feel scattered/burned out, and are familiar with or interested in Notion.

### How is this different from other Notion templates?
- **First comprehensive ADHD template with AI integration**
- Specifically designed for executive dysfunction and ADHD challenges
- Forgiving design (no guilt, built for inconsistency)
- Complete system (not just a planner or database)
- 250+ AI prompts tailored to ADHD needs

### Do I need to have ADHD to create this?
Not necessarily, but:
- Deep understanding of ADHD challenges is crucial
- Consider partnering with ADHD consultants/beta testers
- Do extensive research (lived experience strongly preferred)
- Be authentic and transparent about your connection to ADHD

## Building Questions

### How long does it take to build?
- **Basic template**: 4-6 hours
- **Complete bundle with workbooks**: 20-40 hours
- **Professional polish**: Additional 10-20 hours
- **Total**: Plan for 1-2 weeks of part-time work

### Do I need coding skills?
No! Everything is built using:
- Notion's native features (no-code)
- Basic design skills (Canva level)
- Video recording (Loom or similar)
- PDF creation (Word/Google Docs + export)

### What Notion skills do I need?
You should be comfortable with:
- Creating databases (inline and full-page)
- Setting up properties and relations
- Using views (list, board, gallery, timeline)
- Adding buttons and templates
- Basic formatting (callouts, toggles, columns)

### Can I use Notion's free tier?
- **For building**: Yes, free tier works
- **For selling**: You'll provide a duplicate link, users need their own Notion account
- **For your own use**: Free tier is fine
- **Note**: Some advanced features may require paid tier

### How do I handle AI prompt integration?
Three approaches:
1. **Copy-paste buttons** (easiest): Users copy prompt text into ChatGPT
2. **Database of prompts**: Searchable collection with categories
3. **URL links** (advanced): Direct links with pre-filled text (limited)

**Recommendation**: Start with #1, it's simplest and most reliable.

## Legal Questions

### Do I need an LLC or business entity?
Not required to start, but consider:
- **Sole proprietor**: Simplest (just report on personal taxes)
- **LLC**: Liability protection (recommended once profitable)
- **Check local laws**: Requirements vary by location
- **Consult CPA/lawyer**: Before making big decisions

### What about taxes?
- **Income tax**: Yes, report all revenue
- **Sales tax**: May be required depending on location
- **Gumroad handling**: Gumroad collects VAT for EU sales
- **Hire accountant**: Recommended once revenue is significant

### Do I need terms of service?
**Yes**, include:
- License terms (personal use, not resale)
- Refund policy
- No warranty/liability disclaimer
- Copyright statement
- Use Gumroad's template or hire lawyer for custom

### Can I use copyrighted characters/brands in my design?
**No**:
- Don't use Disney, Marvel, or other protected IP
- Don't copy other creators' exact designs
- Original emoji and generic icons are fine
- Create your own unique visual style

## Troubleshooting

### "I'm overwhelmed building this."
- Break into smaller tasks (30-min chunks)
- Start with simplest MVP version
- Use existing Notion templates for inspiration
- Take breaks (ADHD-friendly advice!)
- Consider hiring help for specific parts

### "I built it but no one is buying."
Check:
- **Traffic**: Are people seeing your sales page?
- **Copy**: Does it address pain points emotionally?
- **Price**: Is it justified with social proof?
- **Trust**: Refund guarantee? Testimonials?
- **Platform**: Try different marketplaces

### "My template is too complex. How do I simplify?"
- Test with non-ADHD users (if they struggle, ADHD users will too)
- Remove features no one uses
- Hide advanced features in toggles
- Create "beginner" vs "advanced" versions
- Watch users try to use it (you'll see friction points)

### "I don't have time to maintain this."
- Set boundaries: Updates every 3-6 months (not weekly)
- Automate delivery: Gumroad handles this
- Community moderation: Hire or find volunteers
- Email automation: Set up sequences once
- Batch create content: 4 hours/month can work
