# 30-Day ADHD Reset Challenge

## Overview
A gentle, forgiving 30-day program designed specifically for ADHD brains. Each day has ONE micro-action that takes 5-15 minutes. No guilt if you miss daysâ€”jump back in anytime!

**Core Principles**:
- Progress over perfection
- Something > Nothing
- No shame for missed days
- Start where you are
- Flexibility is built-in

---

## How to Use This Challenge

### Guidelines
1. **One task per day** - That's it! Don't overdo it.
2. **Set a timer** - Stick to the time limit to avoid overwhelm
3. **Missing days is okay** - Life happens. Just pick up where you left off.
4. **Adjust as needed** - Swap days around if something doesn't fit your situation
5. **Celebrate small wins** - Every single day you complete is worth celebrating!

### What You'll Need
- This workbook (digital or print)
- Notion dashboard (from ADHD Flow OS)
- 5-15 minutes per day
- Self-compassion
- Optional: Accountability buddy or group
